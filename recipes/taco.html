Taco Recipe

Home

Tacos are a simple and delicious meal that can be customized to your taste. Whether you’re using ground beef, chicken, or even a vegetarian filling, tacos offer endless possibilities for flavors and toppings. This recipe focuses on a classic ground beef filling, seasoned with taco spices and served in soft or crunchy tortillas. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a fun taco night with friends and family!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (or chicken)
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade mix)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 8 small taco tortillas (soft or crunchy)
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • Salsa, sour cream, and guacamole (optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it apart with a spatula, until browned (about 5-7 minutes).
  2. Add the taco seasoning and follow the package instructions (usually adding a bit of water and simmering). Stir until well combined and the sauce thickens.
  3. While the meat is cooking, warm the taco tortillas in the oven or on a skillet.
  4. Once the meat is ready, assemble your tacos. Spoon the beef into each tortilla and top with lettuce, shredded cheese, diced tomatoes, onions, and any other toppings you like.
  5. Serve with salsa, sour cream, and guacamole for extra flavor!
